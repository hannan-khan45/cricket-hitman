Jamia Millia Islamia University

Jamia Millia Islamia is one of the most respected Central universities in India. Located in New Delhi, it is known for its academic excellence and diverse campus environment.

Jamia Millia Islamia was established in 1920 at Aligarh during the Indian freedom movement by leaders like Maulana Mohammad Ali Jauhar and Hakim Ajmal Khan, with support from Mahatma Gandhi. It was later shifted to Delhi in 1925 and aimed to promote modern education with nationalist values. In **1988**, it was declared a Central University. Today, Jamia is one of India’s leading institutions known for quality education and research.
