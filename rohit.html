The Hitman – Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is one of the greatest cricketers in the world and a successful captain of India and Mumbai Indians. He is known for his elegant batting style, perfect timing, and ability to score big runs. Rohit holds the record for three double centuries in ODI cricket and the highest individual score of 264 runs. In the IPL, he led Mumbai Indians to 5 championship titles, making them one of the most successful teams. In T20 World Cups, Rohit Sharma has been a key player for India since 2007, when India won its first title. He is among the top run-scorers in T20 World Cup history and has played many match-winning innings. As a captain, he has led India with confidence and strong leadership in ICC tournaments.

Career Achievements

  • Highest ODI Score – 264
  • Three Double Centuries in ODIs
  • Five IPL Titles as Captain
  • Five World Cup Centuries (2019)
